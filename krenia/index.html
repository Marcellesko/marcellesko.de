Krenia

General Information:

Krenia is the main project on my channel and the main focus of my creative attention.
Heavily inspired by HermitCraft, Krenia is a Minecraft SMP (Survival MultiPlayer) that has probably brought some friends together and likely has inspired a few people to be creative and have fun. After having started in early 2021, Krenia has left it's first era after more than a year and is quickly heading towards the second era with new players, new ideas and a brand new world to play and build in.

Players:

After the first era, a lot of new players have joined the project and are ready to construct mini-projects on the server, maybe have fun while putting together a storyline and building their bases. Every single player gets the opportunity to bring their own YouTube Channel to life and upload regular videos.

You can find a list with all the players here.

Datapacks:

Even though Minecraft is undoubtedly a great game, there are a few changes that just make the experience that little bit more enjoyable. Datapacks for example allow little changes like tweaked crafting recipes, different villager trades or slight adaptations in mob or block behavior.

You can find a list of all the Datapacks here.


In case of any questions, you can find out more about Krenia on my Discord Server.

Do you want to join:

If you want to join Krenia, you can either ask on the Discord Server, or send me a private message. I am always happy to see people being interested in the project!
(But keep in mind that you can only join when a new era begins, you are able to make regular YouTube videos and are of a certain age.)
