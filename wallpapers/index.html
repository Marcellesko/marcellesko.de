If you want to have a custom wallpaper that meets your wishes, you can ask me on discord to make you one. If you have a three monitor setup, no worries! If you want to have a wallpaper of your own builds or of a custom map, also no problem! Those custom wallpapers would also be completely free, but please be understanding, that I might not always be able to complete your request.

General Information:

I have made quite a couple of wallpapers from a lot of my projects and builds. There are Wallpapers of Krenia, the Perfect Bases, Raft Story the Asian Temple and even some custom seasonal sceneries.
They are all totally free and you can download them from a google drive folder.

Image Size:

While most one-monitor-wallpapers have a resolution of 4K (3840x2160px), some occasional images are only Full-HD (1920x1080px). If anyone requests a 4K version of those, I can make them and update the gallery, but otherwise it will stay as is.

Currently, the wallpapers are available for single monitor, as well as for dual monitor setups. So, no matter if you have one screen or two, there are wallpapers for both setups.

I haven't made any Wallpapers for three monitors yet, but that might change in the future.
