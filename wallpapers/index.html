If you want to have a custom wallpaper that meets your wishes, you can ask me on discord to make you one. If you have a three monitor setup, no worries! If you want to have a wallpaper of your own builds or of a custom map, also no problem! Those custom wallpapers can also be free depending on the effort they would require. Please be understanding, that I might not always be able to complete your requests.

Image Size:

While most one-monitor-wallpapers have a resolution of 4K (3840x2160px), some occasional images are only Full-HD (1920x1080px). If anyone requests a 4K version of those, I can make them and update the gallery, but otherwise it will stay as is.

Currently, the wallpapers are available for single monitor, as well as for dual monitor setups. So, no matter if you have one screen or two, there are wallpapers for both setups.

I haven't made any Wallpapers for three monitors yet, but that might change in the future.

Usage:

You can you use all of those wallpapers completely freely as desktop wallpapers, backgrounds in any content and everything else that comes to mind. Just don't claim them as your own. Credit isn't needed but appreciated!

* Exceptions:

As mentioned in the custom requests tab, custom requests may end up being an effort worth compensation. Therefore receiving a custom wallpaper may require a small payment. However, every wallpaper downloadable on this site or in the google drive folder is completely free of charge. Once you have the wallpaper I cannot and will not try to charge you anything.
